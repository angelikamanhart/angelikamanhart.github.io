About Me

I am a researcher working in the interdisciplinary field of Computational/Mathematical Biology. I have a double-degree in Mathematics and Molecular Biology and currently hold the position of Associate Professor for Mathematical Biology at the Faculty of Mathematics at the University of Vienna, and an honorary position as Associate Professor in Applied Mathematics at the Mathematics Department of University College London (UCL).

My academic journey →

I believe that good mathematical models allow us to better understand biological phenomena. My work spans a range of scales — from individual proteins and cytoskeletal networks to collective behaviour of cells and tissues.
