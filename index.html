About Me

I am a researcher working at the exciting intersection of mathematics and molecular biology. I hold the position of Associate Professor for Mathematical Biology at the Faculty of Mathematics at the University of Vienna, and an honorary position as Associate Professor in Applied Mathematics at the Mathematics Department of University College London (UCL).

I believe that good mathematical models allow us to better understand biological phenomena. My work spans a range of scales ‚Äî from individual proteins and cytoskeletal networks to collective behaviour of cells and tissues.

I enjoy outreach and public engagement activities and like using art as a means to communicate scientific ideas.
