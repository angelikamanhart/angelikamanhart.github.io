Short CV
 I studied Mathematics until the Diplom (Bachelor + Masters degree) and Molecular Biology until the Bachelors Degree and finished both studies in 2011 with distinction at the University of Vienna. I finished my Phd in 2015 with the group of Christian Schmeiser at the University of Vienna. Until October 2018 I work as a post-doctoral researcher with Alex Mogilner at the Courant Institute at the New York University. After that I worked as a post-doctoral associate with Pierre Degond at Imperial College London. Since September 2019 I am a Lecturer (Assistant Professor) in Applied Mathematics at University College London.
